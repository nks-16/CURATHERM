CuraTherm...

Our organization aims to provide solutions for early detection of breast cancer by employing an innovative and effective method using deep learning techniques. It uses infrared thermography (thermal images) in order to detect and classify the type of cancer that might be present.

We are a group of four young and enthusiastic engineers having a vision to minimize the fatalities caused by breast cancer. This project aims to benefit society by providing easy access for early detection and diagnosis.
